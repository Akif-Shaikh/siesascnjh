Welcome to SIES Journal of Humanities

The SIES Journal of Humanities is a peer reviewed double-blind multi disciplinary annual online research journal (ISSN : 3048-9768 Online). It aims to bring interdisciplinary perspectives in social sciences to reflect on varied paradigms, perspectives, comprehensions and contemplations.

The journal is a step ahead to continue the legacy of the research culture of the institution. In addition, we focus on quality research across the globe serving sustainable development. We provide a robust and careful peer-review process in order to ensure the publication of world-class articles opening novel windows to humanities and making human life happy and cheerful.

About the Journal

The SIES Journal of Humanities represents excellence in academic publishing, providing a platform for scholarly discourse across multiple disciplines within the humanities. Our commitment to rigorous peer review ensures the highest quality of published research.

We welcome submissions from researchers worldwide, fostering international collaboration and knowledge exchange in the humanities field.

Submissions

We welcome high-quality research papers, review articles, and short communications in all areas of humanities. All submissions undergo rigorous double-blind peer review.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Original research papers not published elsewhere
  • Word limit: 6000-8000 words
  • APA citation style
  • Abstract: 150-200 words
  • Keywords: 5-7 terms
